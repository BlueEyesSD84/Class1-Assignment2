Biography

About me. I am originally from Bogalusa, Louisiana(small town about 40 miles north of New Orleans). I lived there until I was about 12 when we moved to Houston, Texas where my Dad got a job with Texas Instruments. We moved to Waller, Texas where I finished high school and went on to join the Navy. I was in the Navy for 20 years achieving the rank of Cheif Petty Officer. I have been to over 30 countries around the world and have been around the tip of Africa and the tip of South America. I then went on to work for the San Diego Superior Court for 10 years in the IT department where I studied and obtained my BS in Software Development from Coleman University. Currently I work for a company remotley that is in Austin, Texas. I am hoping that with this course that I can become a Full Stack Developer and perform work for either a company that I work on my own schedule or a free lance developer. Another option is to possble start my own buiness and hire other developers.
